Butter Chicken Masala

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chilli Powder
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger Garlic
  • Garam Masala
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade and marinate chicken overnight
  2. Prepare the masala and then blend them to make puree for the gravy
  3. Cook marinated chicken in the gravy
  4. Garnish Butter Chicken with cream and serve hot
